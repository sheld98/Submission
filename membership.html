Membership

Eligibility

Membership is open to individuals who attended Musanda Secondary School around 2014, 2015, and 2016.

Fees & Contributions

  • Founder members pay a registration fee of Ksh. 200 and a monthly contribution of Ksh. 200.
  • New members pay a joining fee of Ksh. 500 plus Ksh. 200 per month.
  • Annual membership renewal costs Ksh. 500.

Rights & Responsibilities

  • Members can vote and access welfare services.
  • Members must actively contribute and uphold association values.
