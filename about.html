About Us

Mission:

To foster a supportive and united community that promotes the welfare, equality, and dignity of all members, ensuring opportunities for growth and mutual support.

Vision:

To create a resilient and supportive community where every member thrives through mutual support, shared opportunities, and financial empowerment.

Motto:

United in Support, Empowered for Growth.

The Musanda Alumni 2016 Welfare Association was established to bring together former students of Musanda Secondary School, particularly those from 2014, 2015, and 2016. Our aim is to provide a platform for networking, financial assistance, and community development.
